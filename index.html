Dune: Fremen's Rising

This is a videogame for the subject of Project III at CITM-UPC. Dune: Fremen's Rising is a RTT video game with a perspective camera based on the renowned IP of Dune. This real time tactics game with multiple characters tells the brief story of how Paul Atreides, long after his father’s and Atreides’ allies deaths, lives through a Harkonen siege at the gates of his new Fremen home.

LEGEND OF LANDAMA

This is a videogame for the subject of Project II at CITM-UPC. The game is an RPG Turn-based about a Heroine who has arrived at this town to save the people of Landama from the villains and monsters. She will be helped by a Mage who will follow her where she goes. In the village she will be able to talk with some people and will find enemies to beat fighting in a frenetic Turn-based combat system. Go inside Landama's town and fight, make friends, save them from fear and become the Legend of Landama!

PIRATE SURVIVOR

Pirate Survivor VR is a Shooter VR prototype where you can shot and cut all the projectiles that are throwed to you. This prototype was done for the subject "VR i AR"

APOLLO MISSION

This is a Space videogame for the subject of Physics II at CITM-UPC. Apollo Mission is a game where you have to lunch a rocket and get to the moon, once you have landed to moon, you will have to return safely to earth and land again. Aliens will try to kill because they are going to destroy earth and you are the only who can beat them. Be careful you don't run out of fuel, otherwise, you will get lost in the space.

RACING CAR

This is a Racing car game created with Bullet Physics for the subject of Physics II at CITM-UPC. In this Racing Car game you have to finish 3 laps in the less possible time. Along the circuit you will have to avoid obstacles and show off your driving skills.

ZOMBIE ESCAPE

Zombie Escape is a platformer game by Fake Xeics for the subject of Game Development at CITM-UPC. The game is about a Zombie that needs to escape from the enemies. He needs to find the keys to open the exit doors.
